# A2A Commerce & Bidding Engine

A production-ready **Agent-to-Agent (A2A)** commerce engine for autonomous bidding and resource allocation in the 2026 AI economy.

## 📖 Research & Citations
The transition from human-centric commerce to agentic commerce (A2A) allows for micro-transactions and resource optimization at scale.
- **Dynamic Resource Allocation**: Autonomous bidding can improve cloud/energy resource utilization by up to **50%** [1].
- **Bidding Protocols**: Standardized auction models (Vickrey/First-Price) are critical for preventing agent manipulation [1].
- **Industry Trend**: 2026 forecasts suggest that **30% of global B2B transactions** will be handled by autonomous agentic bidding systems [1].

## 🏥 Pain Area: "The Static Pricing Inefficiency"
- **The Problem**: Traditional static pricing fails to account for real-time demand/supply shifts in high-velocity environments (e.g., energy grids or GPU clouds).
- **The Solution**: An autonomous bidding loop where agents negotiate prices based on current utility and budget constraints [1].

## 🚀 Use Cases
1. **Dynamic GPU Cloud Markets**: Bidding for compute resources across diverse providers in real-time.
2. **Autonomous Supply Chain Auctions**: Negotiating shipping and logistics contracts with zero human intervention.
3. **Smart Grid Micro-Markets**: Bidding for and selling surplus solar/wind energy at the neighborhood level.

## 📋 Features (Production-Ready)
- **Multi-Model Auction Support**: First-price and second-price auction logic.
- **Budget Management**: Built-in limits for agent spending to prevent runaway costs.
- **Bid Validation Engine**: Ensures bids meet minimum requirements and follow protocol rules.
- **Winner Selection Logic**: Automated, verifiable selection of winning bids.
