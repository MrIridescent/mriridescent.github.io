# Bio-Sentinel Carbon Mesh (SDG 15 / AU 2063 Goal 1)
**Status**: Iteration 1.0 (Soil-to-Satellite Alpha)

## 🌌 The Disruptive Vision
Carbon credits are often imprecise and fail to benefit the local African communities managing reforestation. The **Bio-Sentinel Carbon Mesh** is a TinyML-powered, battery-free sensor network deployed across the **Great Green Wall** that monitors soil microbiome health and root-zone carbon sequestration with atomic granularity.

### 🎭 Brand Identity
- **Name**: "Terra-Pulse" (The Heartbeat of the Sahel)
- **Tagline**: "The Soil Never Lies."
- **Logo Concept**: A microscopic root system forming a digital pulse line.
- **Mission**: To turn the "Great Green Wall" into a living, programmable data-mesh for climate justice.

### 🏆 Why This Is Award-Worthy:
1. **Unprecedented Granularity**: Moving from satellite estimations to direct, underground measurement of soil organic carbon (SOC).
2. **Energy Harvesting Autonomy**: Sensors that run on the microbial fuel cell (MFC) energy generated by the soil itself—zero batteries, zero waste.
3. **Sovereign Credit Issuance**: Automated minting of high-integrity carbon credits directly to local community wallets.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Architecture

### 1. Neuromorphic Edge Sensing (Terra-Sense)
- **Core**: Low-power spiking neural networks (SNNs) on-chip to detect microbial activity patterns.
- **Logic**: Real-time analysis of isotopic gas signatures ($CO_2$ and $CH_4$ fluxes).

### 2. Mesh Aggregation (The Root-Net)
- **Engine**: A decentralized LoRaWAN mesh that aggregates data from millions of underground "Sentinels" without needing a cloud backbone.
