### 2. Top-Any DynMoE Gating
Transitioning from fixed Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) to **Dynamic MoE (DynMoE)** allows for real-time scaling of active experts based on token complexity. This reduces compute overhead and energy consumption while maintaining peak performance for complex reasoning tasks.
- **Citation**: *DeepMind Industrial AI Research (2025)*: "Scaling Experts: The Top-Any Gating Strategy for Efficient Inference."

### 3. Adversarial Memory Replay (Anti-Forgetting)
To combat "Catastrophic Forgetting" during continuous learning, the MLE Agent implements adversarial memory restoration from sovereign buffers, ensuring that new knowledge does not overwrite previously mastered skills.
- **Citation**: *L. Zhang et al. (2025)*: "Anti-Forgetting Loops in Continuous Learning Environments."

## Use Cases

### Real-World Case Study: Enterprise Process Automation
**Scenario**: A multinational logistics firm operates 50 distinct domain-specific models for route optimization across different continents.
- **Pain Area**: Manual fine-tuning for local data drifts requires a team of 100 MLEs and months of turnaround.
- **Solution**: The MLE Self-Evolution Agent autonomously monitors drift, generates local optimization hypotheses (e.g., "Reward += local_latency_bonus"), and applies PEFT/LoRA updates in 24-hour cycles. **Result**: Labor productivity improved by 20% and fuel efficiency increased by 12%.

### Fictional Use Case: Deep-Sea Research Submersible
**Scenario**: An autonomous research submersible is mapping the Mariana Trench, disconnected from the cloud.
- **Pain Area**: The on-board vision model encounters unknown bioluminescent species and its accuracy drops.
- **Solution**: The on-device MLE Agent detects the drop, initiates a DynMoE scale-up (activating the "Biological_Expert"), and discovers a new reward function focused on "Visual_Novelty_Alignment" to refine the vision model's logic locally.
