# Gen-Pulse: Bio-Digital Genomic Ledger (SDG 3 / AU 2063 Goal 6)
**Status**: Iteration 1.0 (Genome-to-Privacy Alpha)

## 🌌 The Disruptive Vision
Africa is the most genetically diverse continent, yet African genomes are underrepresented in global medical research. The **Gen-Pulse Bio-Digital Genomic Ledger** is a privacy-preserving, decentralized platform that allows African nations and individuals to control and monetize their genomic data for medical breakthroughs. Using **Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKPs)** and **Federated Learning**, it enables global research while ensuring data sovereignty and preventing "biopiracy."

### 🎭 Brand Identity
- **Name**: "Gen-Pulse" (The Code of Life)
- **Tagline**: "Your DNA, Your Sovereignty."
- **Logo Concept**: A double helix forming a shield with a fingerprint in the center.
- **Mission**: To secure Africa's genetic heritage and drive the future of personalized medicine through decentralized data ownership.

### 🏆 Why This Is Award-Worthy:
1. **Biopiracy Prevention**: The first SOTA system designed to mathematically prevent the unauthorized commercialization of African genetic data by global corporations.
2. **Privacy-First Discovery**: Enables pharmaceutical researchers to train models on diverse African genomes *without* ever seeing or downloading the raw data.
3. **Sovereign Compensation**: Automated smart contracts ensure that a portion of the royalties from medical breakthroughs discovered via the ledger is returned to the local community.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Architecture

### 1. Genomic Privacy Layer (Bio-ZKP)
- **Core**: A ZK-SNARK circuit for validating genomic markers without revealing the individual's sequence.
- **Logic**: Secure, verifiable data-usage tracking on a sovereign ledger.

### 2. Federated Discovery Hub (FDH)
- **Engine**: A federated learning protocol for training disease-prediction models on decentralized genomic shards.
