# AfCFTA Trade-Agent: Swap-Net (SDG 1 / AU 2063 Goal 4)
**Status**: Iteration 1.0 (Trade-to-Liquidity Alpha)

## 🌌 The Disruptive Vision
Intra-African trade is hindered by high tariffs, complex regulations, and a lack of real-time market access. **AfCFTA Trade-Agent** is an autonomous, agent-based platform designed to facilitate **peer-to-peer (P2P) commodity-to-commodity exchange** across African borders. Using **Agentic Diplomacy** and **Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKPs)**, it enables regional producers (e.g., Nigerian maize farmers, South African solar cooperatives) to swap assets directly for digital sovereign credits, bypassing traditional banking hurdles.

### 🎭 Brand Identity
- **Name**: "Swap-Net" (The Liquid Frontier)
- **Tagline**: "Trade Without Borders, Wealth Without Barriers."
- **Logo Concept**: An interlocking exchange of a grain of maize and a solar panel.
- **Mission**: To democratize intra-African trade by unlocking the liquidity of Africa's tangible assets through decentralized exchange.

### 🏆 Why This Is Award-Worthy:
1. **Intra-African P2P Liquidity**: The first SOTA platform that enables real-time, autonomous exchange of physical commodities (e.g., solar energy, surplus maize) for verifiable digital currency.
2. **AfCFTA Regulatory Integration**: Autonomously navigates regional trade agreements (AfCFTA, EAC, ECOWAS) to ensure compliant, frictionless trade.
3. **Hyper-Local Resource Swapping**: Allows a community with surplus energy to swap directly with a community with surplus grain, creating a resilient, self-sustaining regional economy.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Architecture

### 1. Trade-Diplomacy Agent (T-Agent)
- **Core**: A 7B parameter DSLM fine-tuned on AfCFTA and regional trade regulations.
- **Logic**: Autonomous negotiation and contract generation for cross-border swaps.

### 2. Atomic Swap Protocol (S-Net)
- **Engine**: A decentralized bidding and exchange engine that executes atomic swaps between physical asset-tokens and sovereign credits.
