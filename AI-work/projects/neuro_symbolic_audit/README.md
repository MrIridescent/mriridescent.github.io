# Neuro-Symbolic Audit: Truth-Grounded Mission-Critical Suite

## Summary
The **Neuro-Symbolic Audit** suite is a production-grade 2026 framework for **Truth-Grounded AI**. It merges the pattern recognition of neural networks (System 1) with the rigorous, rule-based logic of symbolic AI (System 2). This architecture is specifically designed for high-stakes sectors—such as Nuclear, Defense, and Med-Legal—where model hallucinations are catastrophic. It performs real-time **First-Order Logic** verification to ensure that autonomous decisions are not just statistically probable, but logically valid and legally compliant.

## Research & Citations

- **Truth-Grounded Models**: Frameworks for sectors where hallucinations are catastrophic (Nuclear, Defense, Med-Legal).

## Key Features
- **Neural-Symbolic Feedback Loop**: Neural networks propose actions, while a symbolic auditor (System 2) disposes and verifies them against a formal rule-set.
- **Real-Time First-Order Logic (FOL) Audit**: Automated verification of model outputs against domain-specific logical predicates.
- **Domain-Specific Rule-Sets**: Pre-loaded symbolic rules for Nuclear (core temp, sensor failure), Defense (human-in-the-loop, civilian engagement), and Med-Legal sectors.
- **Constraint Solver**: Recommends adjustments to model outputs to align them with logical validity and safety constraints.
- **Audit History & Traceability**: Immutable logging of all logic-audit cycles, providing full explainability for mission-critical decisions.

## Use Cases
- **Nuclear Core Optimization**: Auditing AI-driven temperature and rod-insertion decisions to ensure they remain within safety bounds.
- **Autonomous Defense Systems**: Enforcing "Human-in-the-Loop" targeting and civilian infrastructure protection via formal logic.
- **Med-Legal Liability Audit**: Ensuring that clinical recommendations are logically consistent with patient narratives and legal statutes.
- **Critical Infrastructure Resilience**: Verifying that autonomous grid-optimization commands are logically valid and do not cause cascading failures.

## Pain Areas & Solutions
- **Pain Area**: Large language models (LLMs) are prone to hallucinations, making them unsuitable for mission-critical environments.
- **Solution**: **Neuro-Symbolic Logic Layers** provide a rigorous, rule-based "reality check" that filters out illogical or unsafe outputs.
- **Pain Area**: "Black box" AI makes it impossible to provide logical explainability for high-stakes decisions.
- **Solution**: **Symbolic Predicate Mapping** provides a transparent, rule-based audit trail for every autonomous action.
