Dynamic MoE Engine (DynMoE)

Architecture by David Akpoviroro Oke (MrIridescent)

1. Top-Any Gating Mechanism

Unlike traditional Top-K methods, DynMoE utilizes a Sigmoid-Threshold Classification. Tokens autonomously 'hire' 0 to N experts based on real-time task complexity.

2. Expert Specialization

Domain-specific sub-networks (Experts) are distilled from frontier models. Each expert focuses on a specific node of the BOLT Knowledge Tree.

3. Self-Evolving Architecture

A Dynamic Optimization Loop monitors expert utility. Underused experts (<10%) are automatically pruned or retargeted to emerging data clusters.
