# Deep Dive 9: Green-Ops & Sustainable AI Lifecycle
## The Path to Carbon-Negative Intelligence (2026-2030)

### 🔬 Research Summary
**Green-Ops** is the practice of optimizing the entire AI lifecycle—from data curation and training to inference—for maximum energy efficiency. By utilizing **NPU-aware scheduling** and **Dynamic Mixture of Experts (MoE)**, energy consumption can be dynamically balanced based on real-time grid availability. Research on **Carbon-Aware Compute (CAC)** shows that shifting 30% of training workloads to non-peak renewable energy periods can reduce the lifecycle carbon footprint of a model by 50%.
