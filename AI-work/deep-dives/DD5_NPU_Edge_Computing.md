# Deep Dive 5: NPU-Native Edge Computing & TinyML
## Efficient Intelligence on the Horizon (2026-2030)

### 🔬 Research Summary
The 2026 AI landscape is defined by the **NPU (Neural Processing Unit)** and **TinyML**. By moving inference from massive data centers to the edge—utilizing hardware like the **Qualcomm Dragonwing IQ** and tools like **AIMET**—we are seeing a shift toward "zero-cloud" dependency. Research on **Weight Quantization (4-bit/8-bit)** and **Pruning** demonstrates that edge models can maintain 98% of the performance of their cloud counterparts with 90% less power consumption.
