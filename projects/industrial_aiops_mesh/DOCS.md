## **2. Operational Documents & Technical Specifications**

### **System Objectives**
- **Predictive Maintenance:** Identify failure patterns across distributed machinery (PLCs, robot controllers).

- **Neuro-Symbolic Logic:** Ensure that autonomous maintenance decisions are both statistically probable and logically valid.

### **Architecture Overview**
The mesh utilizes a **Dynamic Mixture of Experts (DynMoE)** approach, allowing each node (e.g., a robotic arm or CNC machine) to "hire" sub-agents in real-time based on the complexity of the sensor input. The integration of **Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC)** ensures that bidirectional synchronization of model weights is secure against "harvest now, decrypt later" attacks.
