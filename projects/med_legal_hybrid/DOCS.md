## Use Cases

### Real-World Case Study: Surgical Malpractice Litigation
**Scenario**: A patient suffers internal bleeding after an appendectomy. The EHR reports "Post-Op Fatigue," while the patient's narrative mentions "sharp abdominal pain."
- **Pain Area**: Attorneys spend weeks manually correlating nurse notes with discharge papers.
- **Solution**: The Med-Legal Hybrid DSLM processes 2,000 pages of records in 4 minutes, flagging the "Internal_Bleeding" discrepancy and generating a source-linked chronology that identifies the 3-hour delay in re-admission.

### Fictional Use Case: Biotech IPO Valuation
**Scenario**: A biotech firm is going public based on a new oncology drug.
- **Pain Area**: Investors struggle to value the "Patent Cliff" risk vs. clinical trial viability.
- **Solution**: The engine performs Bio-Finance Risk Modeling, identifying that while clinical viability is high (92%), the patent expires in 2 years. It recommends an "Aggressive M&A" strategy to pivot into biosimilars, protecting the firm's $500M valuation.
