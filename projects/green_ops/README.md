# Green-Ops Energy Optimizer

A production-ready **Green AI** optimization suite for carbon-aware model training and inference in 2026.

## 📖 Research & Citations
As AI model energy consumption reaches critical levels, "Green-Ops" has moved from a voluntary ESG initiative to a strict operational requirement for global data centers.
- **Carbon-Aware Scheduling**: Moving workloads to times/locations with lower carbon intensity can reduce emissions by **30-40%** [1].
- **Efficiency Metrics**: Standard tools like `Scaphandre` and `Green Metrics Tool` are now mandatory in 2026 for verifying energy usage at the container level [1].

## 🏥 Pain Area: "The Energy Crisis of AI Scale"
- **The Problem**: High-performance model training and inference consume massive amounts of electricity, leading to both environmental degradation and skyrocketing OpEx costs.
- **The Solution**: A proactive optimization loop that schedules tasks based on real-time grid carbon intensity and utilizes low-power NPU modes where applicable [1].

## 🚀 Use Cases
1. **Sustainable Data Centers**: Automated load-shifting to utilize surplus renewable energy (Wind/Solar).
2. **Eco-Conscious Training**: Dynamically pausing non-critical training jobs during peak grid demand periods.
3. **Green Edge AI**: Optimizing wearable devices to maximize battery life through energy-aware inference.

## 📋 Features (Production-Ready)
- **Carbon-Intensity API Integration**: Mocked connection to global carbon grids.
- **Task Scheduling Logic**: Automatically defers high-energy tasks during high-carbon periods.
- **Energy-Performance Trade-off**: Real-time balancing of inference speed vs. power consumption.
- **Verifiable Reporting**: Generates CSV/JSON reports for sustainability audits.
