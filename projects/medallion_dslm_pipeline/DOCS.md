## **1. Abstract / Research Summary**

The **Medallion DSLM Generation Pipeline** is an automated framework for the systematic creation of Domain-Specific Language Models (DSLMs). In the 2026 AI landscape, the "manual" bottleneck of fine-tuning models is being dismantled by DataOps-centric architectures that treat model specialized training as a manufacturing process.

This project implements the **Medallion Architecture**, which structures data into three refinement layers—**Bronze, Silver, and Gold**. Each layer plays a specific role in turning raw, untransformed data lakes into high-precision AI intelligence. Research indicates that this structured approach ensures 100% data lineage and improves the accuracy of specialized models by up to 24.7% through rigorous normalization and reasoning-path (CoT) distillation.

## **2. Operational Documents & Technical Specifications**

### **System Objectives**
- **Automated DataOps:** Streamline the transition from raw data to DSLM-ready training sets.
- **Data Lineage:** Maintain an auditable trail of all transformations from the Bronze to Gold layers.
- **Expert Reasoners:** Generate synthetic "Chain-of-Thought" (CoT) reasoning paths for student models.

### **Layer Specifications**
1.  **Bronze Layer (Raw):** Landing zone for original data (APIs, SQL dumps, PDFs). No transformations occur here to preserve original intent and auditability.
2.  **Silver Layer (Normalized):** Cleaning and validation. Enforces schemas, removes noise, and handles null/duplicate records.
3.  **Gold Layer (Specialized):** Expert logic injection. Adds domain-specific reasoning and prepares the final dataset for PEFT (LoRA/QLoRA) fine-tuning.
