# AI Diplomacy & Supply Chain Graph: Inter-Sovereign Intelligence Suite

## Summary
The **AI Diplomacy & Supply Chain Graph** is a production-grade 2026 framework for **Inter-Sovereign Intelligence**. It models global interdependencies across minerals, energy, and compute hardware to anticipate global supply shocks and coordinate proactive responses. It serves as a nation's "eyes and ears" for anticipating crisis and supporting trade negotiations through predictive analytics.

## Key Features
- **National Supply Chain Knowledge Graph**: Modeling dependencies across minerals (Lithium), energy (Electricity), and compute (HBM Memory, NPU Silicon).
- **Shock Prediction Simulation**: BFS-style ripple effect modeling of geopolitical or environmental shocks, anticipating the "cascade of failure."
- **AI Diplomacy Strategy Engine**: Automated drafting of trade negotiation strategies based on vulnerability and risk scores.
- **Sovereign Vulnerability Audit**: Proactive identification of single points of failure (SPOF) in the national AI stack.
- **Real-Time Market Signals Integration**: (Simulated) tracking of global signals to update resource risk scores dynamically.

## Use Cases
- **National Security & Defense**: Anticipating semiconductor shortages and securing redundant supply lines for critical defense AI.
- **Trade Negotiation Support**: Providing data-driven leverage points and diversification priorities for diplomatic teams.
- **Energy Grid Resilience**: Modeling the impact of energy shocks on sovereign cloud infrastructure and identifying mitigation strategies.
- **Mineral Resource Management**: Strategic planning for lunar resource mining and deep-space mission management dependencies.

## Pain Areas & Solutions
- **Pain Area**: "Innovation theater" where supply chain modeling is superficial and unintegrated.
- **Solution**: **Predictive Shock Modeling** provides a structural analysis of how failures in one region (e.g., Taiwan) cascade to downstream components (e.g., AI Agents).
- **Pain Area**: Lack of data-driven leverage in complex trade negotiations.
- **Solution**: **AI-Diplomacy Recommended Strategies** identify specific leverage points and diversification priorities based on the supply chain graph.
