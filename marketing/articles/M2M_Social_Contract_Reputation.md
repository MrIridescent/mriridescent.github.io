## 🏗️ The Problem: The "Trust Gap" in Agentic Commerce
As AI agents move beyond the chatbot phase and into autonomous procurement via **Agentic Commerce Protocols (ACP)**, a massive bottleneck has emerged: **Trust**. How does one agent verify that another is not a malicious "predator bot" or an unreliable actor?

## 🛡️ The 2026 Solution: The M2M Social Contract
The **M2M (Machine-to-Machine) Social Contract** is a framework that governs autonomous interaction using three core pillars:
1. **Verifiable Reputation (ERC-8004)**: Every agent carries a tamper-proof reputation score calculated on-chain, based on historical SLA performance.
2. **PQC-Certified Identity**: Utilizing lattice-based cryptography to ensure that agent identities are quantum-safe.
3. **Agentic Trust Vaults**: Secure enclaves for managing the cryptographic "personality" and state of the agent mesh.
